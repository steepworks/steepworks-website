About Steep Works

Civil Engineering & Spider Digger Specialists

Who We Are

Steep Works is a specialist civil engineering company based in Christchurch, Canterbury. We utilize advanced spider digger technology to perform excavation, drilling, trenching, landscaping, and demolition services in challenging terrain where traditional machinery cannot operate.

Our unique multi-legged excavator design and advanced stability controls allow us to navigate steep slopes, wetlands, and delicate ecosystems effortlessly while minimizing environmental impact through reduced soil compaction.

With a team of 2-10 dedicated professionals, we're committed to delivering exceptional results for both residential and commercial projects throughout the Canterbury region.
